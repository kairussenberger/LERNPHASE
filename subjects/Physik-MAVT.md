## The core (4 blocks)

1. **Einführung & Elektrostatik** — fields, potential, capacitors, Gauss's law.
2. **Strom & Magnetismus** — circuits, magnetic fields, induction, Maxwell.
3. **Elektromagnetische Wellen & Optik** — wave equation, polarization, interference/diffraction, geometric optics.
4. **Einführung in die Quantenmechanik** — photons, wave–particle, Schrödinger basics. *(Historically the lightest-weight block — keep it, but front-load 1–3.)*

## Priority order

1. **Past Blockprüfungen with solutions** — you have ~15 (FS16–FS22 + HS20) *plus their Lösungen*. This is the syllabus: drill timed, newest first, and self-check against the Lösung. Highest leverage by far.
2. **Konzeptfragen mit Antworten** — make these a daily active-recall streak; they mirror the conceptual MC part.
3. **Zusammenfassung.pdf** — review + extend into your own condensed sheet (building the sheet *is* studying, and doubles as the exam aid if allowed).
4. **Serien W1–W15** — for blocks where the past-exam drilling exposes weak spots.
