## The core

Design and dimensioning of **machine elements**. Each lecture block is one element family with its own **design recipe** (load → stress/failure criterion → dimension → check). The exam is mostly "apply the recipe to a new configuration," so the win is knowing each element's standard procedure by heart, not deriving anything.

## The 14 element blocks (= the syllabus)

Gears (Zahnräder, V01–V02) · Vordimensionierung & Umlaufgetriebe (V03) · Welle-Nabe-Verbindungen (V04) · Wellendimensionierung (V05) · Toleranzen & Passungen (V06) · Federn (V07) · **Lager & Lagerungen (V08)** · Kupplungen (V09) · **Schrauben (V10)** · Mechanische Mechanismen (V11) · Gestaltung & Fertigung (V12) · Elektromotoren (V13) · Entwicklungsprozess (V14).

**Highest-yield** (recurring, computation-heavy): Zahnräder/Getriebe, Wellendimensionierung, Lager (Last & Lebensdauer), Schrauben.

## Priority order

1. **Übungen with Musterlösung** — these are your best proxy for exam problems. Do each, then reproduce the Musterlösung from blank.
2. **Per-element recipe cards** — build a one-page "given → find → formula → check" sheet for each of the four high-yield elements.
3. **Lecture folien** for the design rationale + the tables/diagrams you'll need to read under time.
4. **Source past exams** and drill them once you've found them.
