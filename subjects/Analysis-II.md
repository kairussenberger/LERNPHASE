## The core

Multivariable calculus + ODEs. Four pillars:

1. **Differential calculus in ℝⁿ** — partial derivatives, gradient, **totales Differential**, chain rule, Taylor, extrema, **Lagrange multipliers**.
2. **Multiple integrals** — double/triple integrals, **Transformationssatz** (Jacobian), polar/cylindrical/spherical.
3. **Vector analysis** — line & surface integrals, vector fields, **Gauss & Stokes**.
4. **Ordinary differential equations** — separable & linear 1st order, higher-order linear, Enveloppen.

The exam is closed-book (assume so until confirmed): you must **reproduce methods from memory at speed**. Integration technique and the DGL solution recipes are the bottleneck.
