## The core

The spring half of linear algebra — typically **eigenvalues & eigenvectors, diagonalization, inner-product spaces & orthogonality, symmetric/orthogonal matrices, quadratic forms, and (S)VD / normal forms**, plus applications. Confirm the exact chapter list against the **Repetition** summaries (they *are* the syllabus in condensed form). The exam rewards fluent, correct matrix computation under time — procedures over proofs.
